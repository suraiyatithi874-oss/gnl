3.1 Fiscal Year Setup

Establish the financial timeline for your organization. This module allows you to define yearly accounting periods, ensuring that all vouchers, ledgers, and reports are anchored to the correct fiscal cycle.

Purpose

To organize and maintain fiscal year records for your company—helping users define yearly accounting periods and ensure proper financial tracking across the ERP.

Navigation:
Menu configuration Fiscal Year Setup

Main Features

Dynamic List View

Real-time table showing Year Name, Start/End Date range, and assigned Company.

Data Controls

Integrated Search bar and Pagination to manage records across multiple years seamlessly.

Instant Actions

Dedicated icons for viewing details, editing existing periods, or removing records.

New Entry

A prominent global button for initializing and configuring upcoming financial years.

How to Use

ADD NEW

Initialize a Fiscal Year

  1. Click the + New Entry button located at the top-right of the screen.
  2. Fiscal Name: Enter a descriptive name (e.g., FY 2024-25).
  3. Date Range: Select the Start Date and End Date from the calendar picker.
  4. Company: Select the specific company this year applies to from the dropdown.
  5. Click Save to commit the record.
UPDATE

Modify Existing Period

1. Locate the year in the list table.

2. Click the Edit icon ().

3. Update the fields and click Save.

DELETE

Remove Record

1. Find the entry you wish to remove.

2. Click the Delete icon ().

3. Confirm the action in the permanent deletion prompt.

VIEW

Audit Details

Click the View icon () beside any fiscal year to open a read-only popup containing the full configuration details for review.

3.2 Department Setup

Define the structural units of your organization. This module allows you to categorize your workforce into functional departments, facilitating better resource allocation and reporting.

Purpose

To help manage internal departments in your organization for clear record-keeping and employee alignment across the system.

Navigation:
Menu configuration Department Setup

Main Features

  • Department Table: A comprehensive list displaying Department Full Names and their corresponding Short Names.
  • Search & Pagination: Tools to quickly locate specific departments and navigate large organizational structures easily.
  • Action Icons: Contextual buttons to View, Edit, or Delete department records instantly.
  • Global Add Button: A dedicated "New Entry" trigger located at the top-right for rapid expansion.

How to Use

NEW ENTRY

Create a Department

  1. Click the + New Entry button at the top-right.
  2. Name: Enter the full name of the department (e.g., Human Resources).
  3. Short Name: Provide a concise abbreviation (e.g., HR).
  4. Click Save to finalize the entry.
UPDATE

1. Locate the department row.

2. Click Edit ().

3. Modify data and save.

VIEW

1. Click View ().

2. Review full details in the popup.

DELETE

1. Click Delete ().

2. Confirm to remove permanently.

3.3 Designation Management

Define and manage the professional roles within your organization. This module ensures that every employee is linked to a specific designation, which serves as a foundation for payroll, reporting, and access control.

Purpose

To organize and manage employee designations within the company—making it simple to create, edit, view, and delete roles used for staffing and organizational reporting.

Navigation:
Menu configuration Designation Management

Main Features

  • Designation List: A centralized grid showing all active roles with their Full Name and Short Name abbreviations.
  • Instant Search: A high-speed search bar to locate specific roles within seconds.
  • Pagination Control: Flexibility to adjust the number of visible entries per page for easier browsing.
  • Interactive Action Icons: Uniform icons for viewing, editing, and deleting records across the list.
  • Rapid Entry: A top-right "New Entry" button designed for quick scaling of job roles.

How to Use

NEW ENTRY

Add a Designation

  1. Click the + New Entry button (top-right).
  2. Name: Enter the full designation (e.g., Senior Accountant).
  3. Short Name: Enter the abbreviation (e.g., Sr. Acc).
  4. Click Save to commit the new role.
UPDATE

1. Find the role in the list.

2. Click the Edit () icon.

3. Modify details and click Save.

VIEW

1. Click the View () icon.

2. A detailed popup will appear for review.

DELETE

1. Click the Delete () icon.

2. Confirm when the system prompts for deletion.

3.4 Holiday Management

Configure the organizational calendar by defining Government, Company-wide, and Special holidays. This ensures accurate attendance tracking, payroll processing, and operational planning.

3.4.1 Govt Holiday

Purpose: To manage government holidays by allowing easy entry, tracking, editing, and removal of holiday records in one central location.
Navigation: Menu Configuration Holiday Setup Govt Holiday
NEW ENTRY
  • Click the + New Entry button.
  • Date: Select the holiday date (e.g., 16-12-2022).
  • Title & Description: Enter the name and details.
  • Dates: Add Effective Start/End dates (optional).
  • Click Save.

Management Actions

Edit: Update holiday fields anytime.

View: Inspect full holiday details.

Delete: Remove outdated entries with confirmation.

3.5 Terms and Conditions

Purpose

Define and manage legal or business terms (e.g., refund/return policies, warranty terms) shown on POS bills, invoices, and quotations to ensure document compliance.

Navigation:
Menu configuration Terms & Conditions Setup

Main Features

  • Manage multiple sets of terms for different business scenarios.
  • Assign specific terms to document types (e.g., POS Sales Bill).
  • Instant status control (Enable/Disable) via toggle switches.
  • Full content management with Rich-Text editing capabilities.
NEW ENTRY
  1. Click the + New Entry button.
  2. Terms Type: Select the document category (e.g., Quotation).
  3. Terms Code: Enter a unique short identifier (e.g., posbill).
  4. Terms Details: Draft the content in the integrated rich-text box.
  5. Click Save.

Management

Publish: Use the toggle switch to make terms live.

Update: Edit the code or text details anytime.

View: Preview the full legal content.
