3. Territory Management

Manage your organization's geographic hierarchy. This section allows you to define the relationship between high-level zones and specific local outlets to ensure precise operational reporting and logistics.

3.1 Zone Setup

Purpose

Purpose: To manage geographic zones by creating, updating, and organizing zone entries for operational or reporting needs.

Navigation:
Menu Territory Management Zone Setup

Main Features

Dynamic Overview

Clear table format displaying all active zones at a glance.

Full Lifecycle Control

Comprehensive CRUD support to View, Edit, or Delete records.

Smart Navigation

Quick search filters and pagination for high-volume datasets.

Operational Workflow

+ New Entry
Create Zone

Enter Name, Code, and Region, then commit with Save.

Modify

Locate record in table, adjust fields, and finalize changes.

Inspect

Launch the detail overlay for a deep-dive view of zone data.

Remove

Execute permanent deletion (requires confirmation popup).

2.2 Region Setup

Hierarchy Level: 01
Operational Purpose

Define major geographical clusters. Regions act as parent containers, aggregating multiple operational Areas under a single unique identifier code.

Navigation:
Menu Territory Management Region Setup

Configuration Steps

01
New Region

Select + New Entry. Assign Name (e.g., Dhaka) and Code (e.g., 01).

02

Use the multi-select tool to link specific Operational Areas to this region.
